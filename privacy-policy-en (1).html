Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and your rights regarding that information.

Who we are

King Media ApS is the data controller for the personal information we process about you. Our contact information is provided at the bottom of this policy.

Information we collect

When you complete a form through our Meta advertisement, we collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to follow up with you about our products and services that you expressed interest in through our advertisement. We will contact you via email or phone based on your submission.

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

Legal basis

We collect and process your information based on your consent, which you provide by submitting the lead form. You may withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us using the details below.

Sharing your information

We may share your information with the following parties:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. – as the technical provider of the lead form
  • Internal team members at King Media ApS who handle follow-up communications

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not share it for purposes other than those described above.

Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. Contact information is typically deleted within 2 years of last contact, unless an ongoing business relationship exists.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting prior processing
  • Object to the processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

Cookies

This page does not use cookies. Meta's advertising platform may use cookies and tracking technologies in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of the page.
